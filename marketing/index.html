Security & Privacy

Your Privacy Is Our Priority

Enterprise-grade security to protect your most sensitive conversations.

🔒

Biometric Authentication

Secure your account with Face ID, Touch ID, or fingerprint. Your data stays locked behind your biometrics.

🔐

Secure Token Storage

Authentication tokens are stored in the device's secure enclave using Expo SecureStore. Never in plain text.

💳

PCI-Compliant Payments

All payments processed through Stripe. We never see or store your card numbers. Apple Pay supported.

🛡

Verified Therapists

Every therapist on our platform is licensed and verified. Your safety and trust come first.

🔥

Firebase Security

Phone verification, social login, and real-time data powered by Google Firebase with strict security rules.

🔄

JWT Authentication

Industry-standard JSON Web Tokens with automatic refresh. Sessions expire and rotate for maximum security.
